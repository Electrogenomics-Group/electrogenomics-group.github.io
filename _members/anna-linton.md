Anna Linton is a postdoctoral data scientist at the William Harvey Research Institute, Queen Mary University of London, working under the NIHR Barts Biomedical Research Centre (BRC). She has a PhD in AI for Medical Diagnosis and Care from the University of Leeds, where she explored the automated analysis of patient-reported outcomes. Anna has a background in AI/NLP, data science and interdisciplinary collaboration.
