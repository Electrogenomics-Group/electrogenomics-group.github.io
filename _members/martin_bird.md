Martin is a Postdoctoral Research Associate in Statistical Genetics at the William Harvey Research Institute, Queen Mary University of London. His research uses large-scale genomic and clinical datasets to investigate the genetic basis of cardiovascular disease and improve molecular diagnosis. He has a particular interest in inherited lipid disorders and arrhythmias, combining rare-variant analysis, genome-wide association studies, polygenic risk scores and functional genomics.
