Lead GWAS variants for QT, JT intervals and QRS duration Nature Communications, 2022

We provide lead variants from multi-ancestry and ancestry specific analyses of the QT, JT intervals and QRS duration from genome-wide association study. We also highlighted potential druggable targets. The data has been made freely available on GWAS catalog (https://www.ebi.ac.uk/gwas/publications/36050321) . This data will be used by other researchers to explore biological mechanisms of disease and develop risk stratification tools.
